\subsection*{Power Series}

\begin{definition*}[Power Series]
A power series centered at \(a\) has the form
\[
  \sum_{n=0}^{\infty} c_n(x-a)^n.
\]
Its convergence is organized by its radius and interval of convergence.
\end{definition*}

\begin{definition*}[Taylor and Maclaurin Series]
The Taylor series of \(f\) centered at \(a\) is
\[
  \sum_{n=0}^{\infty}\frac{f^{(n)}(a)}{n!}(x-a)^n.
\]
The Maclaurin series is the special case \(a=0\).
\end{definition*}
