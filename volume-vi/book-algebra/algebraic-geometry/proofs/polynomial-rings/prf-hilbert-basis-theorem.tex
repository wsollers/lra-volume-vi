\begin{theorem*}[Hilbert Basis Theorem]
If $R$ is a Noetherian ring, then the polynomial ring $R[x]$
is also Noetherian.

In particular, $k[x_1, \dots, x_n]$ is Noetherian for any field $k$.
\end{theorem*}
